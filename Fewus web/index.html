About Us

Fewus Polyclinic is a modern health facility located in Addis Ababa, Ethiopia, dedicated to delivering high-quality, patient-centered medical care. Our focus is on Maternal and Child Health (MCH) services, complemented by general outpatient, minor surgical, and laboratory services. We believe that every mother and child deserves compassionate, reliable, and affordable healthcare. With a team of highly qualified professionals, we provide comprehensive services ranging from antenatal and postnatal care, safe delivery, pediatric consultations, emergency surgical care, to preventive health education.
